Grooming Services

1. Bathing

This service involves cleansing your pet using appropriate shampoos and conditioners suitable for their skin and coat type. It typically includes a thorough rinse and blow-dry, leaving your pet feeling fresh and clean.

2. Haircut & Styling

Professional groomers trim, shape, or style your pet's coat according to your preferences. They use specialized techniques and tools to ensure a neat and aesthetically pleasing appearance tailored to your pet's breed or desired style.

3. Nail Trimming

Groomers carefully trim your pet's nails to an appropriate length, preventing overgrowth, discomfort, or potential injury. They might also file the nails to smooth any rough edges, promoting healthy foot posture and preventing scratching.

4. Teeth Cleaning

Dental hygiene services involve cleaning your pet's teeth and maintaining their oral health. Groomers might use pet-friendly toothpaste, brushes, or other oral care tools to remove plaque and maintain healthy gums, contributing to your pet's overall well-being.

5. De-shedding Treatment

This treatment aims to reduce excessive shedding in pets. Groomers use specialized tools and products to remove loose fur, minimizing shedding and leaving the coat healthier and more manageable. It's particularly beneficial for breeds prone to shedding.
